Strategic Posture

Where STM is investing
  • Catania SiC campus. €5B (€2B state-supported) for fully integrated 200mm SiC fab — substrate, epi, front-end, packaging in one site. First wafers Q4 2025; ramp to 15k wfr/wk by 2033.
  • Crolles 300mm Phase 2. Continued Franco-Italian state-supported MCU + Imaging build-out.
  • Datacenter / AI infra. Multi-year, multi-billion-dollar strategic collaboration with AWS announced Feb 2026 across power management + sensing.
  • Automotive MCU + sensors. Stellar MCU family + ToF / FlightSense sensors design-in pipeline rebuilding into 2026-2027 model years.
What's under pressure
  • Automotive SiC pricing. Wafer prices below $300 per 200mm-equivalent; Chinese IDM (BYD Semi, CRRC, Sanan) capacity expanding aggressively.
  • Industrial inventory. Discrete-power channel inventory still elevated — destocking has stretched into 2026.
  • Margin profile. Q1 2026 GM 33.8% vs 50%+ peak — utilisation charges + price competition.
  • Mobileye / Tesla optical exposure. Indirect, but ToF / FlightSense competitive with smartphone + auto-vision incumbents.
Strategic posture: EXPAND — STM's three structural plays (Catania ramp, AWS datacenter, automotive design-in) are loaded into the H2 2026 / FY2027 trajectory. Margin recovery confirms the platform; missing it pushes the equity narrative back to a defensive cycle.
