Strategic Posture

Where NXP is investing
  • S32 / CoreRide SDV platform. Plug-and-play SDV core for OEMs; 25-30% engineering-efficiency claim, ~2-yr development time. First production 2028; 80% complete end-2026.
  • Imaging radar. New radar awards for imaging radar solutions in Q1 2026; ≥40% global share target.
  • 10G automotive Ethernet. New design wins on 10G auto-Ethernet — anchor for zonal architectures.
  • Process technology. 16nm MRAM auto MCU shipped; 5nm and 3nm automotive-grade chips into 2026-2027 with TSMC.
  • TTTech strategic stake. NXP took strategic stake in TTTech (deterministic-Ethernet IP) — completes the SDV architecture.
What's under pressure
  • Industrial / IoT cycle. Industrial channel destocking has stretched into 2026 — drag on segment growth.
  • Mobile / wallet. Mobile NFC + secure-wallet faces commoditisation; non-core priority.
  • SDV competition. Qualcomm Ride + Mobileye + Renesas R-Car all targeting the same OEM zonal-SDV pipeline.
  • Capacity flexibility. NXP fabless-light model relies on TSMC + GlobalFoundries; capacity allocation risk in tightening AI-driven utilisation environment.
Strategic posture: EXPAND — Q1 2026 +26% stock reaction validates the 2024 Analyst Day commitments. Three hypotheses below test whether NXP can sustain the trajectory through CoreRide first production (2028).
