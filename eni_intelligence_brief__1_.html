Strategy & Intelligence

Satellite valorisation playbook
  • Plenitude — renewable retail energy + EV charging vehicle. Ares €2B 20% stake at €12B+ EV (closed 2025). 5.8 GW capacity post Neoen acquisition; 10 GW target 2028.
  • Enilive — biofuels + sustainable mobility. Triple capacity from 1.7 to ~5 MTPA by 2030. Pengerang Malaysia 650 Ktpa under construction.
  • Eni CCUS — GIP 49.99% strategic partnership; develops + valorises Eni's CCS portfolio (HyNet, Bacton, Ravenna).
  • Versalis — petrochemicals restructuring; reposition as bio-circular chemistry leader.
Africa upstream + LNG anchors
  • Coral North FLNG (Mozambique) — FID achieved 2025; 2nd Coral after Coral South operational since 2022.
  • Indonesia + Malaysia satellites — under development per 2026-2030 strategic plan.
  • Baleine (Côte d'Ivoire) — Vitol 30% sale at €1B; Eni retains operatorship + 70% (residual).
  • Dual exploration model — historic Eni strength: drill exploration, prove resources, sell down stakes to monetise + share risk.

Key Initiatives & Progress

Plenitude — Ares 20% / €2B / €12B+ EV
Closed 2025
Plenitude (Eni's renewable + retail satellite) attracted Ares Management at €12B+ implied EV. 5.8 GW capacity post Neoen (0.76 GW France) acquisition. 10 GW target by 2028. Sets up potential IPO.
Enilive Pengerang biorefinery (Malaysia)
Under construction
650 Ktpa renewable feedstock processing capacity. JV Eni + Petronas + Euglena. Part of Enilive's portfolio to triple biofuel capacity from 1.7 to ~5 MTPA by 2030.
Coral North FLNG (Mozambique)
FID 2025
Second floating LNG unit at Mozambique deep offshore (after Coral South operational 2022). T.EN delivers FLNG vessel. Mozambique fiscal regime stable; security situation primary risk to construction phase.
Eni CCUS — GIP 49.99% partnership
Closed 2025
Strategic partnership with Global Infrastructure Partners. Develops + valorises CCS portfolio: HyNet UK Track-1 cluster, Bacton (UK), Ravenna (Italy), Egypt projects. Specialist capital + Eni execution.
Baleine (Côte d'Ivoire) — Vitol 30% / €1B
Sold 2025
Eni divested 30% of Baleine offshore Côte d'Ivoire to Vitol for €1B. Maintains operatorship + 70% residual. Pure dual-exploration playbook: explore + prove + sell-down to fund.
Versalis bio-circular chemistry
Restructuring
Versalis petrochemicals restructuring underway; reposition toward bio-circular feedstocks. Long-running challenge; structurally lower-margin than Upstream.

HyNet Track-1 + Bacton execution pace gates Eni CCUS commercial returns. Watch UK CCS CfD continuity (per T.EN brief) + EU CBAM Phase 2 expansion to industrial intermediates.
