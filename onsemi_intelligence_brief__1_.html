Strategic Posture

Where onsemi is investing
  • Treo high-margin platform. Analog + mixed-signal family. 60-70% GM target. Q1 +2.5x sequential. Design wins span zonal architectures (10BASE-T1S + SmartFETs), ADAS park assist, humanoid-robotics inductive position sensing.
  • SiC China leadership. ~55% SiC share among new EV models at 2026 Beijing Auto Show. NIO 900V (ES9 flagship), ZEEKR, Vitesco $1.9B LTSA, Magna eDrive — multi-OEM lock-in.
  • AI data center power. +30% QoQ; on track to double YoY. GaN solutions for 48V architectures + AI server PSU. GaN funnel >$1.5B.
  • Czech + New Hampshire SiC fabs. Capacity expansion supported by Magna $40M and Vitesco $250M co-investment.
What's under pressure
  • Inventory build. Q1 inventory build flagged in earnings — discipline required as auto recovers.
  • Non-core exits. Q2 guide includes ~7% drag from non-core exits — restructuring continues.
  • SiC pricing pressure. Chinese IDM (BYD Semi, CRRC) competing on wafer pricing; NIO 900V evolution puts onsemi under continuous tech-pace pressure.
  • European auto destock. Recovery uneven — Europe slower than China.
Strategic posture: EXPAND — Q1 inflection across three growth drivers (Treo, SiC China, AI data center) supports thesis. Three hypotheses below test sustainability through 2026-2027.
