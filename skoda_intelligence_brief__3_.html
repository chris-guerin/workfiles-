VALUE
Core Brand Positioning
Simply Clever — value engineering as differentiator
Škoda is VW Group's most quietly successful brand — consistently delivering strong volumes with high customer satisfaction and VW Group profitability contribution. Built on 'Simply Clever' engineering philosophy, Škoda offers VW-quality vehicles at more accessible price points. The brand's EV strategy (ENYAQ, upcoming Elroq) and India expansion (Kushaq, Slavia) represent the two key growth vectors. Škoda is less exposed to the VW Group crisis than the headline brands — its cost base and market position are fundamentally sounder.
02

Strategy & Intelligence

Strategic Assessment: Škoda's intelligence need is about value-optimised EV technology: how to deliver compelling electric vehicles at price points below VW brand while maintaining the quality and reliability that define the brand. FutureBridge's technology intelligence on affordable EV components (LFP batteries, value-market software) is directly applicable.
EV Transition — ENYAQ & Beyond
  • Škoda ENYAQ: MEB-based EV SUV — among Europe's best-value premium electric SUVs
  • ENYAQ Coupé RS: performance variant positioning Škoda above its traditional value niche
  • Elroq (2024): compact EV SUV below ENYAQ — critical for volume EV expansion
  • Future platform: MEB+ and SSP compatibility for next-generation Škoda EVs
  • Value engineering focus: Škoda delivers MEB technology at lower price than VW ID.4
India — The Volume Growth Engine
  • MQB-A0-IN: India-specific platform developed at significantly lower cost than global platforms
  • Kushaq (SUV) and Slavia (sedan): launched 2021-2022; strong market reception in India
  • Škoda Auto Volkswagen India: the JV responsible for delivering 1 million units in India by 2030
  • Component localisation: 95%+ local content for India models — genuine cost competitiveness
  • Future India EVs: exploring electric versions of India models for 2027+ — with affordable LFP batteries
The 'Simply Clever' EV Formula
  • Škoda's engineering philosophy is pragmatic over flashy — translate this to EV: best range per euro, not best 0-100
  • Customer base values reliability, practicality, and value — these translate well to EV if price is right
  • The key technology question: when do LFP batteries make MEB-platform cars viable at €25-30K?
  • Škoda Elroq targets exactly this price point — technology choices will define its competitiveness
  • FutureBridge: LFP battery cost trajectory and value-market EV software intelligence
FutureBridge Entry Points
  • Škoda DigiLab: digitalisation and future mobility innovation team (Mladá Boleslav + Prague)
  • Technology Academy: Škoda's internal training and technology scouting capability
  • India strategy team: market intelligence on India EV landscape and competitive positioning
  • FutureBridge's emerging market energy and mobility intelligence is a direct fit for India work
  • CEE automotive supply chain intelligence: relevant for Škoda's Czech manufacturing ecosystem
