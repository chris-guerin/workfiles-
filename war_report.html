One rule before every call: The client is already aware of the conflict. Do not explain it to them. Open with what FutureBridge knows that they do not. The insight hook is the only reason they will give you 30 minutes.
Sales script · Energy · Europe · Shell / BP / TotalEnergies · C-Suite, CSO, VP Strategy

"We track technology adoption across every segment of the energy system. What we're seeing in the last 60 days is that European utilities are making grid storage technology selections at a pace we haven't seen since 2011. The decisions being made now will lock in technology choices for a decade. That's what I wanted to talk to you about."

"Most of the technology selection conversations we're observing are happening before the TRL data supports them. Three of the four grid storage technologies being actively procured will not reach the performance targets they're being specified against. That's a very expensive mistake to make now."

"When your team is evaluating storage technology options for the next investment cycle — what data are they working from? Is it vendor-provided or independently assessed?"

FutureBridge provides an independent technology readiness ranking for grid storage, scored against your specific performance requirements and market context. We deliver in 8 weeks. The output is a decision brief your board can act on — not a 200-page report. We've done this for three European utilities in the last quarter.

We run an 8-week technology landscape sprint. Your technology team gets the assessment; your leadership gets the decision brief. No ambiguity.
