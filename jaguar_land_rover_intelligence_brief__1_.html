Strategic Posture

Where JLR is investing
  • Range Rover Electric (2026 launch). Core franchise battery EV — first volume electrification of the Range Rover line. Critical to Reimagine strategy execution.
  • Jaguar Type 00 GT. Reveal 2026, deliveries 2027. Tri-motor 1,000hp, 800V architecture, 400-mile target, $130k+. UK-built. Brand-reset proof point.
  • JEA architecture. Jaguar Electrified Architecture (800V) supporting Type 00 GT + future Jaguar EV portfolio.
  • £18B investment plan to FY28. Multi-year capex programme spanning electrification + plant modernisation + software.
  • Reimagine strategy. Net-zero 2039; modern luxury + electrification + sustainability + enterprise pillars.
What's under pressure
  • Cyber incident Q3. Production shutdown disrupted FY 2026; recovery + insurance claim sequencing material.
  • US tariffs. Premium SUV exposure to US market makes JLR particularly tariff-sensitive.
  • Legacy Jaguar wind-down. Planned exit of legacy Jaguar models leaves brand-revenue gap until Type 00 GT delivers volume from 2028.
  • Polarising rebrand. Jaguar 2024 rebrand (logo + visual identity) drew backlash; designer Gerry McGovern departed. Type 00 GT must justify the reset.
Strategic posture: RECOVER — FY 2026 is a transition year; recovery + electrification thesis loaded into FY 2027-2028. Three hypotheses below test Range Rover Electric, Jaguar Type 00 GT, and FY 2027 EBIT recovery.
