How to use the toolkit

01
Paste your API key
Add your Anthropic key in the sidebar of any AI tool. Stays in your browser. Nothing sent to any server.
02
Open an account plan
Select any of 36 companies. Plan generates in ~30 seconds and caches locally. Clear cache to regenerate.
03
Read the chain
Performance → strategy → pain points → initiatives → technologies → weak signals. Follow the logic, not just the conclusions.
04
Use the FB map & brief
Every initiative maps a capability to a hypothesis. Use that in the meeting. Download the client brief to send after.
