SW-01

Software delivery — three-architecture stack by 2028

Hypothesis. The three-architecture VWG software strategy (CARIAD Global + Rivian SDV West "RV Tech" + Xpeng SDV East) delivers a functional unified Group Software Stack across brands by 2028, with the VW ID.1 as the first production vehicle running Rivian-derived software (target: late 2027).

CARIAD restructured Oct 2025 from developer to integrator. €12B invested in CARIAD since inception. Rivian JV tensions: Audi Q8 e-tron and A4 e-tron delayed 1+ year to mid-late 2028. Porsche K1 postponed indefinitely.

WNTBT — All conditions hold
  • VW ID.1 first production with Rivian-derived software achieved late 2027
  • Audi Q8/A4 e-tron launch holds for mid-late 2028 (no further slippage)
  • CARIAD operates as integrator (not core developer) consistently through 2028
  • Porsche K1 timing publicly re-committed by end-2027
  • No major Group Software Stack reset announced before end-2027

CHN-01

China defence — units >2.5M / BEV share >10% by 2027

Hypothesis. VWG stabilises China unit sales above 2.5M/year and recovers BEV market share above 10% by 2027 via CMP platform (China-only, launch 2026) and China Electronic Architecture (CEA) with Xpeng (deploy 2027).

2024 China sales 2.93M (vs 4.23M peak 2019). BEV deliveries -34.5% YoY H1 2025. China operating margin 4.2% H1 2025. BYD at 34% China EV share. 130 brands competing. 2025 declared "rebuilding year" by VWG China boss.

WNTBT — All conditions hold
  • China unit sales hold ≥2.5M/year through 2027
  • VWG BEV share in China recovers to ≥10% by FY2027
  • CMP platform launches 2026 with at least 2 nameplates
  • Xpeng CEA deploys 2027 in lead model
  • China operating margin holds ≥4% through 2026-2027 transition
  • No further BYD-driven share cliff (>5pp loss in any single year)

COST-01

German manufacturing reset — €10B savings + maintained capacity by 2027

Hypothesis. VWG executes €10B cost savings programme by 2027 while maintaining ICE capacity (Golf/Tiguan Wolfsburg backordered — weekend overtime running) and transitioning BEV factories (Zwickau/Emden production halted Oct 2025 citing demand slowdown).

€10B group cost-savings programme. Workforce restructuring negotiated with IG Metall. MEB platform amortisation across group volume hinges on EU BEV demand recovery (HYP_VWG_EV_001).

  • €10B cost-savings run-rate achieved by end-2027 (≥€7B by end-2026)
  • ICE Wolfsburg / Tiguan / Golf production sustained without further cuts
  • Zwickau and Emden BEV production restarts and reaches >70% prior utilisation by H2 2026
  • No major IG Metall industrial action forced in 2026
  • MEB platform amortisation per-vehicle cost falls ≥15% from 2024 base by 2027

BAT-01

PowerCo battery independence — competitive Salzgitter cells by 2028

Hypothesis. PowerCo (VWG battery subsidiary) achieves cell production at competitive cost from Salzgitter gigafactory by 2028, reducing dependency on CATL/Samsung SDI for ID-family models. Group targeting >200 GWh capacity by early 2030s (Valencia + Salzgitter + Canada).

PowerCo first cells targeted 2025-26. Current ID-family supply: CATL/SK On (ID.4, ID.7). Multi-chemistry roadmap: LFP for entry, LMFP for mid, NMC for premium.

WNTBT — All conditions hold
  • PowerCo Salzgitter delivers first commercial cells by end-2026
  • Cell cost-per-kWh within 10% of CATL benchmark by 2028
  • >200 GWh group capacity (Salzgitter + Valencia + Canada) committed and on schedule by 2028
  • ≥30% of ID-family cells supplied internally (PowerCo) by 2028
