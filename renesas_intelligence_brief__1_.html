Strategic Posture

Where Renesas is investing
  • R-Car Gen 5 / X5H SDV. First 3nm multi-domain auto SoC — ADAS + IVI + Gateway in one. Centerpiece of Renesas SDV positioning vs NXP CoreRide / Qualcomm Ride / Mobileye.
  • Altium platform. A$9.1B (~$5.9B) Aug 2024 acquisition — PCB design + lifecycle. Vehicle for industrial software monetisation alongside silicon.
  • Wolfspeed equity + SiC capacity. Pre-petition $2B deposit converted to equity + secured convertible debt; 16.85M shares + board designee. SiC capacity anchor for power-electronics roadmap.
  • Transphorm GaN. Acquired GaN-on-Si power technology — complements SiC for higher-frequency power apps.
  • Industrial / IoT compounding. +32% YoY Q1 2026; secular AI / industrial automation tailwinds.
What's under pressure
  • Q2 2026 margin pressure. Q2 guidance higher revenue but lower margins (FX + mix + cost). Yen volatility a key driver.
  • SDV competitive pressure. Qualcomm Ride + Mobileye + NXP CoreRide all targeting the same OEM zonal-SDV pipeline.
  • Wolfspeed integration risk. Renesas now sits on the Wolfspeed cap table and board — equity-method losses + restructuring execution risk.
  • Acquisition digestion. Multi-year integration sequence (IDT, Dialog, Altium, Transphorm, Wolfspeed) requires sustained management bandwidth.
Strategic posture: EXPAND — Q1 2026 confirms underlying growth. Three hypotheses below test SDV positioning, Altium monetisation, and Wolfspeed SiC value capture.
