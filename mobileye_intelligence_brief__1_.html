  • Mahindra design wins. SuperVision + Surround ADAS — India market expansion.
  • Mentee Robotics acquisition. Closed Q1 2026 — embodied AI / robotics extension of vision stack.
What's under pressure
  • SDV-platform competition. Qualcomm Snapdragon Ride + NXP CoreRide + Renesas R-Car all targeting same OEM zonal-SDV pipeline; Mobileye competitive on vision-first stack only.
  • China exposure. Limited China OEM penetration vs domestic alternatives (Horizon Robotics, MetaX).
  • Intel parent overhang. Intel still majority shareholder — periodic share-secondary risk.
  • Tesla FSD precedent. Tesla FSD rollout sets a benchmark — vision-only argument validated, but raises bar for Mobileye consumer ADAS narrative.
