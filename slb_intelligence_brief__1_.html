02 · Strategic posture (May 2026)

SLB's three-leg strategy: (a) Core OFS (Well Construction + Reservoir Performance) — the historical cycle-exposed business now under Middle East disruption pressure; (b) Production Systems / ChampionX — a deliberate pivot into less-cyclical, capital-light production chemicals + artificial lift + emissions tech, lifted to ~35% of revenue mix post-merger; (c) Digital & Integration — the Lumi + Delfi platform layer, with new agentic capabilities (Autonomous Directional Drilling, geosteering AI) and major customer wins (ONGC enterprise deployment).

The Q1 2026 EPS preannouncement — citing Red Sea logistics bottlenecks and NOC project recalibration — exposes the cost of SLB's Middle East concentration. CEO commentary frames the disruption as transient, but the FY2026 trajectory now turns on whether normalisation lands by H2 2026.
