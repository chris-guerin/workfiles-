02

Strategic Posture

Where Continental is investing
  • Premium 18"+ tires. Strong growth in 18-inch-plus premium tire segment driving Q1 2026 margin uplift. APAC expansion + EV / all-season demand.
  • Realignment completion 2026. ContiTech sale planned 2026 = final step toward pure-play tire. Aumovio (Sept 2025) + OESL (Feb 2026) already executed.
  • Mid-single-digit unit growth. Sustained >10% Tires operating margin while pursuing volume in premium / EV-ready segments.
  • Industrial recovery in H2 2026. ContiTech profitability improving despite weak market — operational flexibility ahead of sale.
What's under pressure
  • Tariff burden. Q1 2026 Tires margin lifted "despite burdens from tariffs" — US trade environment a continuing headwind.
  • FX headwinds. EUR strength + non-EUR exposure compresses both Tires and ContiTech reported sales.
  • ContiTech sale execution. 2026 sale process pending — value realisation depends on industrial-cycle timing + buyer interest.
  • Equity ratio compression. Aumovio spin took equity ratio to 26.3% — balance-sheet flexibility narrower.
Strategic posture: REPOSITION — Continental is mid-transformation from diversified Tier-1 to focused tire pure-play. Three hypotheses below test transformation completion, Tires margin durability, and premium-tire / APAC growth thesis.
