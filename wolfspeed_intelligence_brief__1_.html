Strategic Posture

Where Wolfspeed is investing
  • Mohawk Valley 200mm utilisation. $1.2B JV with NY State at SUNY Polytechnic Marcy site. Q1 2026 contribution $97M. Critical to gross-margin recovery.
  • AI data center / aerospace / ESS pivot. CEO Robert Feurle leading diversification beyond EV softness. AI data center revenue +50% sequential.
  • 300mm SiC roadmap. Industry-first single 300mm SiC wafer Jan 2026. Long-term cost-curve advantage if scaled — currently single-wafer milestone, not commercial.
  • Renesas strategic relationship. 16.85M shares + convertible debt + board designee (Aris Bolisay, Feb 2026). Embeds Renesas as anchor demand customer.
What's under pressure
  • EV demand weakness. Soft demand from automotive customers expected through fiscal 2026. Pre-restructuring EV-heavy book underperforms.
  • Underutilisation costs. Q1 GM -26% non-GAAP; -39% GAAP. Manufacturing fixed-cost burden persists pending Mohawk Valley ramp.
  • Q2 guidance below consensus. $150-190M vs $232M expected. Recovery profile delayed.
  • Equity-holder dilution + restructuring overhang. Pre-bankruptcy shareholders heavily diluted; investor narrative recovering.
Strategic posture: CAUTION — Wolfspeed's three structural hypotheses below are all credible but execution-dependent. The next 4 quarters are decisive.
